My Newly Discovered Songs Where to listen to Music? How does music help us?

For others, they may say that they only enjoy listening to music. That it's just a pass time. However, music has much more to this. It has been proven that listening to music provides us health benefits. It may be physically and mentally. To know more, let's dive more into it.

It reduces stress.

This is by far the first advantage of listening to music. May it be through dancing, singing along, or just vibing; listening to music is no lie a great way to reduce our negative feelings and thoughts. With the help of it, we gain positive mood instead.

It benefits your heart.

Yes, this has been proven by scientists. Music can help you move more. In addition, music has also been proven to change our breath rate, heart rate, and blood pressure depending on the music's tempo and rhythm.

It helps improve our studying process.

Although listening to songs with lyrics may be distracting to others, it is still known to be effective to make studying easier. Jazz and classical music are the best ones to listen to when studying.

It can improve memory.

In relation to learning, music is also effective in improving memory. Associating lessons or anything with music and singing it to memorize is actually effective!

It boosts exercise performance.

Have you noticed that people in gyms usually listen to music on their earphones while exercising? If yes, the reason behind this is that music, as I mentioned earlier, helps boost and improve mood. And as a result, people who listen to it tend to be more energetic and dynamic.


Source: Stanborough, R. M. J. (2020, April 1). The Benefits of Listening to Music. Healthline. https://www.healthline .com/health/benefits-of-music#physical-benefits
