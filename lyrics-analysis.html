My Newly Discovered Songs Where to listen to Music? How does music help us?

2!3!

Artist: BTS

This BTS song is about staying together through challenges in life. We can't always expect everything to go exactly as planned in life. Unexpected challenges and problems must be faced from time to time. These help us in becoming stronger than we are now. Besides that, these contribute to the strengthening of our bonds with those we care about. Despite this, we must learn to look on the bright side of things and believe that everything will be fine soon. Also, let us be grateful to those with whom we shared our problems and struggles, who supported us when we needed it, and who stood by our side.

More than Words

Artist: Extreme

This song basically tells you to express your feelings to the person. You should do it through your actions, not just your words, as it will be more true and sincere. You don't have to literally tell someone how much you love them if you express your feelings through actions because actions speak louder than words. Even if you ignore all words of endearment , your feelings for the person will be clear. Furthermore, actions show how much you truly care about that person.

MAPA

Artist: SB19

Yes, it's this one! A song dedicated solely to our parents! This one encourages us to be grateful to them and acknowledge their sacrifices and everything they have given us. Apart from that, this song also teaches us to work hard and dedicate our achievements to them. And above all, this also tells us to love our parents with all our hearts.

Let Her Go

Artist: Passenger

This song tells us that we only see the value of a person when we lose them. We sometimes forget how much they mean to us or how much they've given us. And this is the message of the song. This song teaches us to learn how to value and appreciate those people that are close to our hearts. We should always acknowledge their sacrifices for us and be grateful for whatever they give us. Remember, no one can predict how long we will last.
